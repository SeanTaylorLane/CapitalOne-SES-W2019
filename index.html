Regular commuters

68.29% of trips were made by regular commuters while a whopping 31.71% of trips were impromptu walk-up purchases! Maybe the number of impromptu purchases speaks towards Metro's marketing efforts or their station placement.

Weekend riders

Not surprisingly, most trips are made during the week. However, it is still significant that about 27.06% of trips are taken during the weekend.

Weekday Ride Times

The peak time for bike sharing during the week is between the times of 4pm-8pm. Surprisingly, there is an increasingly high volume of trips from 8am to 8pm. Why are there more trips in the afternoon? Do some people who bike home don't bike to work?

Weekend Ride times

The peak time for bike sharing over the weekend is between the times of 12pm and 4pm. It seems riders don't wake up early to bike on the weekend!

Average Distance per Day

The average distance per day is fairly even throughout the week. However, there is a surprising dip on Saturday. Why do weekend bikers avoid Saturday? Is there a conflict between leisure activities that doesn't exist on Sunday?

Average Duration per Season

The average trip duration per season is very even, with the highest average in the Spring and the lowest in the Fall. Does this have anything to do with temperature, or is culture playing a role here?
